# EduLaluan Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how EduLaluan ("EduLaluan", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data in accordance with the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia**.

By using EduLaluan, you consent to the processing of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. About EduLaluan (Data User)

EduLaluan is an educational platform that provides education and career navigation resources for Malaysian communities.

For purposes of the PDPA, EduLaluan acts as a **Data User** (the entity that processes personal data). Our website is hosted by third-party infrastructure providers such as Netlify, who may process data on our behalf as **Data Processors**.

---

## 2. Types of Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

### (a) Personal Data Provided by You

This may include:

* Name
* Email address
* Contact details
* Messages or feedback submitted through forms
* Account registration information (if applicable)

### (b) Personal Data Collected Automatically

This may include:

* IP address
* Browser type and device information
* Pages visited and interaction data
* Access times and referring websites
* Cookie identifiers

### (c) Personal Data from Third Parties

Where relevant, we may receive data from:

* Authentication providers (if login integrations are used)
* Analytics providers
* Services you voluntarily connect to EduLaluan

You may choose not to provide certain personal data, but this may limit your ability to use some features.

---

## 3. Purpose of Processing Personal Data

In accordance with the **General Principle of PDPA**, we process personal data only for lawful and relevant purposes including:

* Providing educational resources and platform functionality
* Improving website performance and user experience
* Responding to inquiries or support requests
* Sending updates where consent has been provided
* Maintaining platform security
* Meeting legal and regulatory obligations

We will not process your personal data for unrelated purposes without obtaining further consent.

---

## 4. Disclosure of Personal Data

In accordance with the **Disclosure Principle**, we do not sell personal data.

We may disclose personal data to:

* **Service providers and contractors** assisting with hosting, analytics, or technical operations
* **Professional advisors** where necessary
* **Regulatory authorities or law enforcement** where legally required
* **Successors in business transactions** such as restructuring or asset transfers

All third parties are required to protect personal data consistent with PDPA requirements.

---

## 5. Transfer of Personal Data Outside Malaysia

Your personal data may be transferred to locations outside Malaysia where our service providers operate infrastructure.

Where such transfers occur, we take reasonable steps to ensure:

* Comparable data protection standards are applied
* Appropriate contractual safeguards exist
* Transfers comply with PDPA cross-border transfer requirements

By using our services, you consent to such transfers where necessary for service operation.

---

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies consistent with the **Notice and Choice Principle**.

Cookies may include:

* **Essential cookies** – required for core functionality
* **Analytics cookies** – help us improve performance
* **Preference cookies** – remember user settings

You may disable cookies through browser settings. Some services may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

---

## 7. Data Security

In accordance with the **Security Principle**, we implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal data against:

* Unauthorized access
* Loss or misuse
* Modification or disclosure

However, internet transmission is not completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 8. Data Retention

Under the **Retention Principle**, personal data is retained only as long as necessary for:

* Fulfillment of stated purposes
* Legal compliance
* Dispute resolution
* Enforcement of agreements

When no longer required, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

---

## 9. Data Accuracy

Under the **Data Integrity Principle**, we take reasonable steps to ensure personal data is accurate, complete, and up to date.

Users are encouraged to notify us if their personal data requires correction.

---

## 10. Your Rights Under PDPA

Under the **Access Principle**, you have the right to:

* Request access to your personal data
* Request correction of inaccurate data
* Withdraw consent (where applicable)
* Limit processing where legally permissible

Requests may be subject to:

* Identity verification
* Reasonable administrative fees (where allowed under PDPA)

Requests can be submitted using the contact details below.

---

## 11. Children's Personal Data

If personal data of minors (under 18) is collected, consent should be provided by a parent, guardian, or lawful representative.

If we become aware that personal data was collected without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.
