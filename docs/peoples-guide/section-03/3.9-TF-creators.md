## Content Gap Notice

As of March 2026, Malaysian tax and finance content has grown significantly — but notable gaps remain:

* **Business tax in simple language** — content explaining SST, CP204, and withholding tax for small business owners is sparse outside of professional firm blogs
* **Zakat combined with financial planning** — very few creators bridge Islamic finance obligations (zakat, faraid) with practical financial literacy in one voice
* **Tax for gig workers and freelancers** — e-Filing and CP500 guidance for the self-employed is underserved compared to salaried worker content
* **BM-language investment education at depth** — most in-depth investing content defaults to English, leaving BM-dominant audiences underserved beyond surface-level tips
* **Tax literacy for B40 households** — most personal finance content targets M40 and above; content addressing the specific financial situations of lower-income households is limited

**If you create or know of a channel covering any of these gaps, please suggest it below.** EduLaluan will review and add verified resources.
