## Content Gap Notice

As of March 2026, Malaysian tech services content is relatively well-served in gadget reviews and Shopee/Lazada selling tips — but several meaningful gaps remain:

* **Telco complaint and rights education** — independent creators explaining MCMC escalation, number porting, and consumer rights in the telco space in BM are rare; most Malaysians only find this information when they have a problem
* **Broadband comparison for non-KL areas** — most speed test and ISP review content focuses on Klang Valley, Penang, and JB; rural and East Malaysian broadband coverage is almost entirely absent from creator content
* **Cloud and hosting for SMEs in BM** — cloud adoption for Malaysian SMEs would benefit from accessible BM-language guides on choosing and using hosting; current content is almost entirely English and developer-focused
* **BNPL and consumer credit education** — as BNPL grows rapidly in Malaysia, independent creator content explaining the risks, credit implications, and how to manage it responsibly is sparse
* **Cross-border e-commerce consumer rights** — as Temu, AliExpress, and international platforms grow, what rights Malaysian consumers have and how to seek recourse for cross-border purchases is almost entirely unaddressed
* **E-wallet security education in BM** — scam prevention for e-wallet users specifically (QR fraud, OTP scams, phishing) needs more dedicated BM-language creator content beyond official PDRM and MCMC channels

**If you create or know of a channel covering any of these gaps, please suggest it below.** EduLaluan will review and add verified resources.
