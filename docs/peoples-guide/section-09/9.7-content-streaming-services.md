## Audience Tabs

### 🧒 Simple

**What is streaming?**

Streaming means watching or listening to content over the internet without downloading it.

You pay a monthly subscription and access thousands of movies, shows, or songs.

Popular in Malaysia:

* Netflix for international series
* Viu for Korean dramas and Asian content
* sooka for live sports and Malaysian content
* Durioo Plus for Islamic children's content
* Deezer or Spotify for music

Most offer a free trial. Most allow cancellation at any time.

## Overview

Streaming has replaced physical media for most Malaysians. The market has two layers: global platforms with localised catalogues, and Malaysian or regional platforms built for local content needs.

This page covers video, music, open source alternatives, and tools for managing subscriptions and migrating between platforms.

| Tool | What It Does | Technical Level |
| --- | --- | --- |

## Part 5: Geo-Blocking and VPN Considerations

### What Geo-Blocking Is

Streaming platforms restrict content by region due to licensing. A show available on Netflix US may not appear on Netflix Malaysia. Catalogues differ by country.

### VPN for Geo-Blocked Content

| Factor | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Terms of service | Most platforms prohibit VPN use; enforcement varies |
| Legal status in Malaysia | Using a VPN is not illegal in Malaysia. Bypassing geo-blocks violates platform terms of service, not Malaysian law |
| Detection | Major platforms actively block known VPN IP ranges |
| Reliability | Free VPNs fail frequently; paid VPNs (Mullvad, ProtonVPN) work more consistently |

VPN-based geo-blocking workarounds are widely practised. They are a terms-of-service violation, not a legal offence.

### Legal Alternatives to VPNs

Before using a VPN, check whether a legal option exists:

* K-dramas on Netflix US are often available on Viu Malaysia legally
* Anime blocked on Netflix Malaysia is often on iQIYI or WeTV
* US network shows may be on local broadcaster catch-up services

## FAQ

**Q: Is Tidal worth the extra cost over Spotify in Malaysia?**
For casual listeners on standard earbuds: no. The quality difference requires good headphones, a DAC/amp, and attentive listening. For audiophiles with quality audio equipment: yes. Try the free trial with your actual setup before deciding.

**Q: Can I share a Netflix account with people in other households?**
Netflix's password-sharing policy now ties accounts to a single household. Family members in different homes need their own account or to be added as an "extra member" at additional cost. Check Netflix Malaysia's current policy for the latest enforcement details.

**Q: Is Bandcamp available in Malaysia?**
Yes. Bandcamp is accessible in Malaysia for both purchases and streaming. Artists receive a larger revenue share than on Spotify or Deezer. Bandcamp Fridays (one Friday per month) waive Bandcamp's cut entirely, sending 100% to artists.

**Q: Does Jellyfin require technical knowledge?**
Moderate knowledge is needed. You need a home server or NAS, basic networking understanding, and willingness to troubleshoot. For non-technical users, a local media player like VLC is simpler for playing files from an external drive.

**Q: Does sooka carry EPL matches?**
sooka carries selected sports rights that change with broadcasting contracts. Confirm current EPL coverage at sooka.my before subscribing specifically for that content.
