- 9.7 Streaming and Digital Content Services
- 9.8 Termination, Complaints and Escalation
  - 9.8.1 Telco Termination and Complaints
    - File report with MCMC first
    - CC MCMC when sending termination notice to provider
    - MCMC: aduan.skmm.gov.my, 1-800-18-0300
  - 9.8.2 Financial Services Termination and Complaints
    - All financial institutions (banks, insurance, financial services) are under BNM supervision
    - Complaints handled by the institution's dedicated complaints unit, not regular customer service
    - If unsatisfied with a declined claim, escalate to the Ombudsman for Financial Services (OFS)
    - Insurance agent misconduct: report to MII, PIAM (general insurance) or LIAM (life insurance)
    - BNM TELELINK: bnmtelelink@bnm.gov.my, 1-300-88-5465
  - 9.8.3 E-Commerce and Consumer Service Termination
    - KPDNHEP: e-aduan@kpdn.gov.my
    - Tribunal for Consumer Claims Malaysia (TTPM): official@ttpm.gov.my
  - 9.8.4 Escalation CC Contacts Quick Reference

    | Issue Type | Primary | CC |
    |---|---|---|
    | Telco | Provider complaints unit | aduan.skmm.gov.my |
    | Banking and finance | Institution complaints unit | bnmtelelink@bnm.gov.my |
    | Insurance claim dispute | OFS | PIAM / LIAM |
    | Consumer / e-commerce | TTPM | e-aduan@kpdn.gov.my |
