## About This Page

This page lists Malaysian content creators, official agency channels, and media resources that help the public understand digital safety, cybersecurity, privacy, AI literacy, and ethical technology. This is a living page. If you know a creator or channel that belongs here, use the **"Suggest a resource"** button at the bottom.

**Note:** EduLaluan does not endorse any individual creator's technical or policy views. Digital safety and privacy information changes rapidly — always verify against current official sources (MCMC, CyberSecurity Malaysia, PDPA Commissioner). Be cautious of creators promoting paid "digital security services" without transparent credentials — verify professional background before engaging.

## Content Gap Notice

As of March 2026, Malaysian digital safety and ethical tech content has some strong official voices — but significant gaps remain across independent creator content:

* **BM-language cybersecurity education** — most cybersecurity creator content in Malaysia is in English; Malay-dominant audiences are substantially underserved beyond official PDRM and MCMC channels
* **AI literacy in BM** — AI is transforming Malaysian life and work, but accessible AI literacy content in BM — beyond surface-level chatbot coverage — is almost entirely absent
* **PDPA and data rights education** — the Personal Data Protection Act affects all Malaysians who interact with private organisations, but independent creators explaining data rights in plain language are rare; most people learn about PDPA only when something goes wrong
* **Ethical tech and supply chain education** — topics like e-waste, tech labour rights, and algorithmic accountability are covered by international outlets but have almost no Malaysian independent creator presence
* **Digital rights for marginalised communities** — content specifically addressing digital safety for domestic violence survivors, LGBTQ+ individuals, elderly users, and OKU communities is nearly absent despite these groups facing amplified digital risks
* **Deepfake and synthetic media detection in BM** — as deepfakes become more accessible and more prevalent in Malaysian political and social contexts, creator content on recognition and verification in BM is critically needed

**If you create or know of a channel covering any of these gaps, please suggest it below.** EduLaluan will review and add verified resources.
