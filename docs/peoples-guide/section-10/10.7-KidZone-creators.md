## Content Parents Should Approach Carefully

Some content needs supervision:

* Gaming streamers
* Influencer lifestyle content
* Unverified "study hacks"
* Product review channels targeting children
* Algorithm driven short videos

Check:

* Who made it
* Why it was made
* Whether it teaches or only entertains

Teach children to ask questions. Not just consume.

## How to Evaluate a Creator (Teach Kids This)

Teach children three simple checks:

**1. Is it true?**
Check if other trusted sources say the same.

**2. Is it safe?**
No dangerous behaviour. No harmful advice.

**3. Is it useful?**
Does it teach something real?

If not, skip it.

Simple rule:

**Learn more. Scroll less.**
