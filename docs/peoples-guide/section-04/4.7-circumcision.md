## Page Purpose

A community-contributed resource to help adults — especially those new to Islam (reverts) — navigate circumcision with confidence. Submissions are experience-based, not medical advice.

---

## Female Sunat — Editorial Framing (Critical)

Islamic circumcision for females (sunat perempuan) involves only a gentle needle prick on the clitoral hood. There is:
- No cutting
- No removal of tissue
- No lasting anatomical alteration

This is categorically different from Female Genital Mutilation (FGM). FGM is harmful, condemned by international health bodies, and is not Islamic practice. This distinction must be stated clearly and prominently on the page.
