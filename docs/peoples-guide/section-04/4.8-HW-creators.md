## About This Page

This page lists Malaysian content creators, official agency channels, and media resources that help the public understand health, wellbeing, mental health, nutrition, and medical services. This is a living page. If you know a creator or channel that belongs here, use the **"Suggest a resource"** button at the bottom.

**Note:** EduLaluan does not endorse any individual creator's medical views. Health information from creators should always be verified against KKM, your medical officer, or a registered health professional. Be particularly cautious of creators promoting specific supplements, treatments, or products — check that any health product is registered with NPRA at npra.gov.my.

## Content Gap Notice

As of March 2026, several gaps remain in Malaysian health content:

* **Specialist health conditions in BM** — most in-depth content on specific conditions (cancer, rare diseases, chronic illness management) defaults to English, leaving BM-dominant audiences underserved
* **Healthcare navigation for the elderly** — content helping older Malaysians and their caregivers navigate public healthcare, chronic disease management, and elder care is sparse
* **Verified traditional medicine education** — the traditional medicine space is dominated by product promotion; independent creators providing evidence-informed guidance on what is safe and regulated are rare
* **Health content for persons with disabilities (OKU)** — accessible health content (subtitled, signed, or adapted) for the OKU community is significantly underrepresented
* **Mental health in the workplace** — while general mental health content is growing, content specifically addressing occupational mental health, burnout, and EAP in a Malaysian context remains limited

**If you create or know of a channel covering any of these gaps, please suggest it below.** EduLaluan will review and add verified resources.
