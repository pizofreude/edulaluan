## Content Gap Notice

As of March 2026, government services content in Malaysia is one of the least-served areas by independent creators despite being among the highest-need topics for ordinary Malaysians:

* **Government services in plain language** — most procedural content comes from official agencies whose communication style is formal and bureaucratic; independent creators translating this into accessible guides are rare
* **SPA exam preparation** — while some communities exist, structured high-quality exam prep content for PTD and other SPA exams is sparse compared to SPM or university entrance content
* **Non-BM government services content** — Tamil and Mandarin-language content navigating government services is significantly underrepresented; many Malaysian communities rely on informal word-of-mouth instead
* **eGovernment and digital services literacy** — MyDigital ID, MySejahtera, and MyGOV tutorials for older and less tech-savvy Malaysians are limited; what exists tends to target younger, already digitally-literate audiences
* **Scholarship bond experiences** — honest, first-hand accounts of what it is like to be bonded to the civil service, how bond deferments work, and how scholars navigate early career decisions are almost entirely absent from public content

**If you create or know of a channel covering any of these gaps, please suggest it below.** EduLaluan will review and add verified resources.
